Google OAuth App

Openclaw Calender

A personal AI assistant integration by Nevit

Openclaw Calender is a Google OAuth application operated by Nevit that enables the OpenClaw AI assistant to read and display Google Calendar events for personal productivity purposes.

What this app does

Openclaw Calender connects your Google Calendar to the OpenClaw AI assistant, allowing it to:

  • Read your calendar events to provide daily briefings and reminders
  • Display upcoming appointments in the assistant's morning/evening summaries
  • Answer questions about your schedule in natural language

Data usage & privacy

  • Calendar data is accessed only on your behalf and solely to generate personal summaries
  • No calendar data is stored permanently or shared with third parties
  • Access tokens are held securely on your own infrastructure and never transmitted externally
  • The integration is strictly personal — one user, one account

This app's use of Google Calendar data is limited to displaying information to you, the authenticated user. It complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Permissions requested

  • Google Calendar (read-only) — to read your calendar events

Who operates this app

Openclaw Calender is operated by Nevit, a personal productivity project based in Grenchen, Switzerland. It is not a commercial product and is not available to the general public.
